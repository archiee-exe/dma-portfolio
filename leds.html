THE LED ARCH

human machine interaction

This was our intro project we worked as a group of to make a roman arch fillied with colorful LEDs that would get PTSD to a roman solider as they walk on our DIY swtich.



Artist Statement

For this project we mostly focused on learning the fundacmentals of soldering and electronics. We used 9 LEDs in parallel series combination. 3bulbs were in series combined with two more strands making it Parallel-series combination with the help of resistors. We created our own switch using aluminium foils and soft wraping foam. It will be used as foot steps. For the final presentation of the LEDs i arrenged them in the constellation of Leo.
